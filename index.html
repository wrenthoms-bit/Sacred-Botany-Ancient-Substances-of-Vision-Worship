The Flow of Eras

CHRONOLOGY OF SACRED VISIONS

A chronological ledger of humanity's deepest chemical partnerships, preserved in stone art, architectural artifacts, and written scroll.

~ 7000 BCE
Sahara Cave Paintings

THE ORIGIN OF TEONANÁCATL

Cave paintings in Tassili n'Ajjer, deep in the Algerian Sahara, depict mysterious humanoid deities wearing mushroom-head garbs or holding sprouting fungi, establishing some of the earliest documented entheogen interactions.

~ 4000 BCE
Neolithic Asia

HEMP CLOTH & REMEDY SCROLLS

Early Neolithic cultures in the Yangshao and Longshan regions of China utilize cannabis as hemp fibers for vital fishing cords, sails, and basic medicinal recipes compiled in ancient herbal texts.

~ 3400 BCE
Mesopotamian Sumer

DISCOVERY OF THE JOY PLANT

The Sumerians design initial clay tablets highlighting the cultivation of the opium poppy (*Papaver somniferum*). They name the wondrous plant *Hul Gil*, meaning the "joy plant," passing its medical secrets directly down to ancient Babylonia and Egypt.

~ 1000 BCE
Pre-Classic Mesoamerica

THE MUSHROOM STONE AGE

Sacred Mesoamerican mushroom stones carved inside Highland Guatemala and parts of Mexico prove the intense, cross-generational worship of psilocybin during holy gatherings and civic coronations.
